1. Terms of Use

By using Pizza Board, you agree to the following:

  • Pizza Board is provided "as is" without any warranties.
  • You are responsible for the content you create and share.
  • The service is intended for collaborative drawing and should not be used for illegal activities.
  • We reserve the right to modify or discontinue the service at any time.

2. Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Pizza Board is designed to be as private as possible:

  • No Data Collection: We do not store your drawings, IP addresses, or personal information on our servers.
  • Peer-to-Peer: Collaboration is handled directly between participants using WebRTC technology.
  • No Accounts: You do not need to create an account to use the service.
  • Local Storage: Temporary session data may be stored in your browser's local storage or memory, but it is never transmitted to us.

3. Third-Party APIs & Services

To provide real-time collaboration, we use the following third-party services:

  • PeerJS: A library that facilitates WebRTC peer-to-peer data connections. When you use "Together Mode," PeerJS uses its public cloud servers to help establish a direct connection between you and your collaborators.
  • Unpkg: Used as a Content Delivery Network (CDN) to serve the PeerJS library.
